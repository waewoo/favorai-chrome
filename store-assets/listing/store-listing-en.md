# FavorAI - Chrome Web Store Listing (English)

## SUMMARY (132 chars max)
🧠 AI bookmark manager: smart duplicates, dead links, top-visited insights, and semantic reorganization.

---

## LONG DESCRIPTION
Are your bookmarks a graveyard of forgotten links, duplicates, and folders that made sense three years ago? FavorAI is the intelligent Chrome extension that fixes all of that automatically using the AI provider you already have.

With one click, FavorAI scans your bookmark library, detects problems, queries your AI, and proposes a clean, semantically organized structure. You review the proposed changes, select what to apply, and hit confirm. That's it.

CORE FEATURES

🔍 Smart Duplicate Detection
Automatically finds duplicate bookmarks beyond exact URL matches: http/https variants, www vs non-www, tracking parameters, redirects to the same final page, and similar article content across domains when advanced link checks are enabled.

🔗 Dead Link Verification
Detects 404s, timeouts, and unreachable servers so you can purge links that no longer exist.

🤖 AI-Powered Semantic Reorganization
Proposes a clean, logical folder hierarchy with intuitive names based on your bookmark content. You choose the AI provider.

⚙️ Granular Analysis Controls
Choose which checks to run before the AI step: dead links, redirects, and content duplicate checks can be toggled separately.

📊 Folder Insights
When you select a folder, FavorAI shows the bookmark count and an estimated token cost so you can size the analysis before you launch it.

📋 Review Before You Commit
Every proposed change is listed in a checklist: folder creations, moves, renames, deletions. Approve all, some, or none.

🕰️ Session History and Rollback
Every session is saved locally. Made a mistake? Roll back instantly with one click.

🕰️ Forgotten Bookmarks
A dedicated tab surfaces bookmarks you have not visited in months (3 months, 6 months, 1 year, or never). Review each one and decide: visit, keep, or delete individually or all at once.

💡 Smart Bookmark Placement
When saving a new bookmark, FavorAI suggests the best folder based on the page content, powered by your AI. Accept the suggestion, ask for an alternative, or choose manually.

🌍 English and French Interface
The UI adapts to your browser language automatically.

⚡ Two Reorganization Modes
- Minimal: fixes clear semantic mismatches while preserving your existing folder structure.
- Complete: full redesign. The AI rethinks your entire bookmark hierarchy from scratch.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Configure your AI provider. Enter your API key - it stays on your device.
2. When saving a page, FavorAI instantly suggests the right folder. Accept, get an alternative, or pick manually.
3. For a full cleanup, click "Reorganize all bookmarks" to scan smart duplicates, optionally verify redirects and dead links, and restructure everything.
4. Use the analysis options to control which checks run before the AI step.
5. Review every proposed change in the checklist - approve all, some, or none.
6. Use the History tab to review or roll back any session instantly.
7. Open the Forgotten tab to surface bookmarks left untouched for months and clean them up in one pass.

YOUR AI PROVIDER

FavorAI works with the AI service you configure yourself, whether it runs locally or in the cloud. If you already use a custom endpoint, you can plug it in as well.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY

- No FavorAI servers. No backend, no database, no user accounts.
- Your API key is stored locally in chrome.storage.sync, encrypted by Chrome, and never transmitted to FavorAI.
- Bookmark data is sent only to your chosen AI provider - you control where it goes.
- No telemetry, no analytics, no tracking of any kind.
- Uninstalling the extension permanently removes all stored data.
