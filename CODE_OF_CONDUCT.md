# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to stay useful, respectful, and easy to work with.
Everyone who participates in this project is expected to treat others with dignity and courtesy.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and clear.
- Focus on the work, not the person.
- Give feedback that helps others improve.
- Respect different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Deliberate disruption of discussion or collaboration
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

If behavior crosses the line, maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation.
